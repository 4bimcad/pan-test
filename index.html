COLON FREE ZONE

The Colon Free Zone is a free zone that has more than 70 years of experience in world trade, it is the largest zone in the American continent and the main container center in Latin America.

With 1,064.5 hectares, located on the Caribbean coast of Panama in the province of Colón, within the city of Colón near the recently expanded Panama Canal and around 2,000 companies that carry out intense commercial activities operate.
